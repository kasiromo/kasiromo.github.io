Welcome to Kasi's Music Directory!

I, like many others, love to listen to music. For me, music not only serves as a form of entertainment, but also serves as a grounding presence, motivational force, or spirit uplifter on those tougher days.

And while the scope of music that I listen to is not limited to these two genres, I must say that these two genres — k-pop and metal — hold incredibly special places in my heart.

So, without further ado, please feel free to browse the K-Pop and Metal navigation tabs found at the top of this page. From there, you'll be introduced to some of my favorite artists within those respective genres. Hell, you might even discover a new musical artist to give a listen to!
